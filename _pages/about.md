Hi, it's Zafer [/zɑ·fɛɾˈ/](http://ipa-reader.com/?text=z%C9%91%C2%B7f%C9%9B%C9%BE%E2%80%99&voice=Filiz).

I am a cybersecurity and IT operations professional based in Tallinn, Estonia. My work sits where security governance, infrastructure security, operational resilience, and practical defensive engineering meet.

I started from the technical side of IT, with software development, systems administration, networks, and infrastructure operations, and later moved deeper into cybersecurity, risk, compliance, and continuity. That background shapes how I think about security: controls should not only look correct on paper; they should survive real systems, real constraints, and real failure modes.

This site is where I write about cybersecurity, infrastructure, software, digital systems, and the occasional broader question that sits around them.
