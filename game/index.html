Fatigue

A SIEM economics simulator

The scenario

You are the newly appointed Security Manager. Your organisation has no visibility, no detection capability, and no analysts. Threats are already probing your environment. Build a Security Operations Centre that can detect, triage, and respond — before the board loses confidence.

How to play

Enable log sources to collect telemetry. Hire analysts to triage the alert queue. Research detection engineering to write rules. Manage your budget, reputation, and analyst fatigue. Meet your SLA targets for detection and response time — or face termination.

SLA targets

Mean Time to Detect for critical services must stay below 15 minutes. Standard services must stay below 30 minutes. Mean Time to Respond must stay below 60 minutes. Three consecutive SLA breaches or five total breaches will end the game. Weekly SLA fines use ƒ200 + ƒ100 per existing violation + random ƒ0–ƒ300 jitter.
