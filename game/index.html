Fatigue

A SIEM economics simulator

The scenario

You are the newly appointed Security Manager. Your organisation has no visibility, no detection capability, and no analysts. Threats are already probing your environment. Build a Security Operations Centre that can detect, triage, and respond before the board loses confidence.

How to play

Enable log sources to collect telemetry. Hire analysts to triage the alert queue. Research detection engineering to write rules. Manage your budget, reputation, and analyst fatigue.

SLA targets

Metric Target Hard limit
MTTD Critical (severity 3) 15 minutes 18 minutes
MTTD Standard (severity 1-2) 30 minutes 36 minutes
MTTR (all incidents) 60 minutes 75 minutes
Missed critical incident Always a hard violation
Missed standard (2+) Hard violation

First soft breach triggers a warning with grace. Second soft breach or any hard breach counts as a violation. Five total violations or three consecutive end the game.
